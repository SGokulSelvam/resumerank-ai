Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ResumeRank AI ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website at resumerank-ai.onrender.com.

By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Resume Files: Files you upload (PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT) are processed in real-time for ATS analysis only. They are never stored on our servers after analysis.
  • Job Descriptions: Text you paste is used only for analysis and is not stored.
  • IP Address: Used only for rate limiting (3 free checks per day). Not linked to any personal identity.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve our service (page views, feature usage).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide AI-powered ATS resume analysis
  • Enforce rate limits on free tier usage
  • Improve our website and services
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense

4. Data Storage & Security

Your resume is NEVER stored. All resume files and job descriptions are processed in memory and immediately discarded after analysis. We do not maintain any database of user resumes.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information.
